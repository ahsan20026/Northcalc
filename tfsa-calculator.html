About TFSA Room

How TFSA room works:
Your contribution room accumulates every year on January 1st if you are: (1) a Canadian resident, (2) at least 18 years old, and (3) have a valid SIN. Unused room carries forward indefinitely.
Annual TFSA Limits:
2009–2012: $5,000  |  2013–2014: $5,500
2015: $10,000  |  2016–2018: $5,500
2019–2022: $6,000  |  2023: $6,500
2024–2025: $7,000
⚠️ Over-contribution penalty:
The CRA charges 1% per month on any over-contribution amount. Always verify your room on My CRA Account before contributing.
